**1. What Info We Grab**

We get info in a couple of ways:

a. What you give us:

*   Your name, email, or number
*   Stuff when you sign up
*   Payment info (if needed)
*   Anything you message or tell us

b. What we get automatically:

*   Your IP address and browser
*   Device type and when you visit
*   Pages you looked at and links you clicked
*   Cookies and stuff

**2. How We Use Your Info**

We use your info to:

*   Keep the site running
*   Process payments and help you out
*   Send you updates or deals (if you're cool with it)
*   See how the site's doing and make it better
*   Follow the law

We will not sell your info to anyone.

**3. Cookies and Stuff**

We use cookies to:

*   Remember your settings
*   See how many people use the site
*   Show you cool stuff

You can change cookie settings in your browser, but some things might not work right if you turn them off.

**4. Sharing Your Data**

We might share your info, but only if we have to:

*   With companies that help us (like for hosting, email, or payments)
*   If the law makes us
*   If the biz gets sold

Everyone we share with has to keep your info safe.

**5. Keeping Your Data Safe**

We try to keep your data safe, but no online service is perfect.

**6. Your Rights**

You might have the right to:

*   See, fix, or delete your data
*   Stop us from sending you stuff
*   Get a copy of your info
*   Say no to some things

**7. Other Sites**

We might link to other sites, but we're not in charge of their privacy stuff. Check their policies.

**8. How Long We Keep Data**

We keep your data as long as we need to, or as the law says.

**9. Policy Changes**

We might change this policy. Any changes will be on this page.
